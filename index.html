What is this?

This is a fun app to check if your birthday is lucky or not. Enter your birthday and lucky number and we will tell you if your birthday is lucky or not.

Working Process

First, we will add all the digits of your birthday. Then we will check if the sum is divisible by your lucky number. If it is divisible, then your birthday is lucky.
